sweet sorrow

> a voice beside you, in your quiet hours

[https://sweet-sorrow19910310.vercel.app](https://sweet-sorrow1991310.vercel.app)

 What is this?

sweet sorrow💧 is a quiet web experience for the moments when you feel alone.

Press *Listen*. A voice arrives — not to fix anything, not to advise.
Just to be there, beside you, while the screen slowly shifts from deep blue to the soft pink of dawn.

The whole journey takes about 50 seconds. Long enough to breathe. Short enough to fit into any quiet hour.

 Why I made it

I first imagined this as something for emergencies — a voice for people in disaster shelters, or in hospital waiting rooms.

But I realized: in a real emergency, no one has the room in their heart to listen to a small voice.

The loneliness that actually needs company is the quieter kind. The 3 a.m. kind. The "I'm fine" kind. The loneliness inside ordinary days, when nothing is wrong but nothing is right either.

So this is for those hours. Not for crisis. For the long, soft, in-between time that most of us live in.

 How to experience it

1. Open the page in any browser (works on phones too)
2. Press **Listen**
3. Stay for as long as you'd like

The background slowly turns from night to dawn while you listen. You can press **Stop** at any time.

Tech

- Single-file HTML, no framework
- Canvas animation for the flowing water and dawn transition
- Plain `<audio>` with iOS-safe unlock for mobile playback
- Deployed on Vercel
